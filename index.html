🩸 Bleeding Control

▼
  1. Apply direct pressure with clean cloth
  2. Elevate the injured area above heart level
  3. Continue pressure for 10-15 minutes
  4. If bleeding doesn't stop, seek medical help

🔥 Burns Treatment

▼
  1. Cool the burn with running water for 10-20 minutes
  2. Remove jewelry and tight clothing
  3. Cover with sterile, non-stick bandage
  4. Do NOT apply ice, butter, or ointments
  5. Seek medical help for severe burns

🦴 Fracture Care

▼
  1. Immobilize the injured area
  2. Apply ice packs to reduce swelling
  3. Do NOT try to realign the bone
  4. Seek immediate medical attention

❤️ CPR Steps

▼
  1. Call emergency services (108)
  2. Place person on firm surface
  3. Give 30 chest compressions (100-120/min)
  4. Give 2 rescue breaths
  5. Continue until help arrives

🫁 Choking Relief

▼
  1. Encourage coughing
  2. Give 5 back blows between shoulder blades
  3. Give 5 abdominal thrusts (Heimlich)
  4. Repeat until object is expelled
  5. Call emergency if unsuccessful
